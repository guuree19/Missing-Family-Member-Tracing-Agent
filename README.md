
# Missing-Family-Member-Tracing-Agent

## Overview

Missing Family Tracing Agent is a humanitarian AI-powered platform designed to support family tracing and reunification workflows for people affected by conflict, disaster, displacement, migration, or emergencies.

The platform helps families submit tracing requests, organize case information, upload supporting documents, and track case progress through a privacy-focused workflow.

This project was developed as part of the Kaggle AI Agents Capstone under the **Agents for Good** track.

## Problem

Millions of people worldwide become separated from family members due to war, disasters, forced displacement, migration, and humanitarian crises.

Families often face challenges in:

* Reporting missing loved ones
* Organizing tracing information
* Tracking case status
* Managing supporting documentation
* Accessing tracing resources

---

## Solution

The Missing Family Tracing Agent provides a structured and privacy-conscious environment for:

* Missing person case intake
* Document collection
* Case organization
* Case status tracking
* Humanitarian workflow support
* AI-assisted information management

The platform is designed to support affected families, volunteers, and humanitarian workers.

## Human Oversight

The platform does not verify identities, confirm family matches, or conduct reunification activities independently.

All matching, verification, and reunification decisions must be performed by trained human personnel.
